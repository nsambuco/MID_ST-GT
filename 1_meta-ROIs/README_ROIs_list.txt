This document describes the anatomical content of the two NIfTI files, winA_Clust.nii and winO_Clust.nii. These files contain meta-analytically derived regions of interest (ROIs) used for extracting BOLD signals in reward-related fMRI task phases.
The ROIs were primarily based on a published meta-analysis of the Monetary Incentive Delay (MID) task, which did not include the datasets in this study. The distinction between anticipation and outcome phases allows for the granular investigation of cue-versus-outcome-driven responses.
